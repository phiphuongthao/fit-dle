NECK PAIN

AUGUST 30, 2020

Neck pain and problems in the neck are very common and affect millions of people each year. Neck pain, also sometimes referred to as Cervicalgia, may be present in many forms and be caused by various conditions. For example neck pain related to the cervical spine may be caused by a pinched nerve, whiplash, degenerative disc disease, spinal stenosis or arthritis in the neck.
